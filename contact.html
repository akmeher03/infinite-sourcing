Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

MOQs vary by product category and manufacturer. Generally, MOQs range from 200-1000 units per style/color. We can discuss specific requirements during consultation and work with you to find solutions that match your needs.

How long does the sourcing process take?

The timeline depends on product complexity and customization. Typically, sampling takes 2-3 weeks, and production takes 4-8 weeks after approval. We'll provide a detailed timeline specific to your project during consultation.

Can I request samples before placing an order?

Absolutely! We encourage sample review before production. We can arrange existing product samples or develop custom samples based on your specifications. Sample costs and shipping are typically charged but may be credited toward larger orders.

Do you handle custom designs and specifications?

Yes, we specialize in custom sourcing. Whether it's specific fabric compositions, unique designs, custom sizes, or private labeling, we can work with our manufacturing partners to deliver exactly what you need.

What payment terms do you offer?

Standard terms typically include a deposit at order confirmation and balance before shipment. For established clients, we offer flexible payment terms including letter of credit options. Specific terms are discussed during the quotation process.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide. We handle all export documentation and can arrange shipping via sea or air freight based on your timeline and budget. We work with reliable freight partners to ensure safe delivery to your destination.
