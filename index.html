About Us

Your Trusted Partner in
Premium Home Textiles

Founded in 2019, Infinite Sourcing has rapidly emerged as a leading sourcing partner and exporter of premium home textiles from India. Based in Delhi, we connect global brands with the finest manufacturers, combining traditional weaving expertise with cutting-edge technology.

We specialize in creating bespoke textile solutions for global retailers, boutique brands, and hospitality chains. Every product that leaves our facility reflects our unwavering commitment to quality, sustainability, and design innovation.
