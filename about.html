Our Journey

Infinite Sourcing was founded with a simple vision: to simplify the textile sourcing process while maintaining uncompromising quality standards.

What began as a small operation connecting local retailers with quality manufacturers has grown into a comprehensive sourcing partner serving clients across the globe. Over the past 15 years, we've built relationships with over 50 vetted manufacturers and served more than 200 clients in the home textiles industry.

Our deep understanding of textile production, combined with our commitment to quality and ethical practices, has made us a trusted name in the industry.

Our Mission

To provide businesses with seamless access to premium home textiles through transparent, reliable, and sustainable sourcing practices that create value for all stakeholders.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted textile sourcing partner globally, known for quality excellence, ethical practices, and building lasting relationships with manufacturers and clients alike.
