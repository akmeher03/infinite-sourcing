Our Commitment

Sustainability

Building a more sustainable future through responsible textile sourcing and ethical manufacturing partnerships.

Why It Matters

Textiles with Purpose

The textile industry has a significant environmental footprint. At Infinite Sourcing, we believe that beautiful home textiles shouldn't come at the expense of our planet or the people who make them.

We're committed to partnering with manufacturers who share our values—those who prioritize sustainable materials, ethical labor practices, and environmentally responsible production methods.

Our Approach

Four Pillars of Sustainability

Sustainable Materials

We prioritize organic cotton, recycled fibers, and responsibly sourced materials that minimize environmental impact.

  • Organic cotton sourcing
  • Recycled polyester options
  • Natural fiber alternatives
  • Low-impact dyes

Ethical Manufacturing

Every manufacturer in our network meets strict standards for fair wages, safe working conditions, and worker rights.

  • Fair wage compliance
  • Safe working conditions
  • No child labor policy
  • Regular social audits

Water Conservation

We partner with facilities that implement water-saving technologies and responsible wastewater treatment.

  • Water recycling systems
  • Efficient dyeing processes
  • Wastewater treatment
  • Reduced water consumption

Energy Efficiency

Our manufacturing partners invest in renewable energy and energy-efficient production to reduce carbon footprint.

  • Solar power adoption
  • Energy-efficient machinery
  • Carbon footprint tracking
  • Renewable energy goals
Verified Standards

Certifications We Support

We work with manufacturers holding internationally recognized certifications.

GOTS

Global Organic Textile Standard ensures organic status of textiles from harvesting through manufacturing.

OEKO-TEX®

Tests for harmful substances ensuring textiles are safe for human use and the environment.

GRS

Global Recycled Standard verifies recycled content and responsible production practices.

Fair Trade

Ensures fair prices and wages for workers in developing countries.

BCI

Better Cotton Initiative promotes sustainable cotton farming practices worldwide.

SA8000

Social accountability standard for decent working conditions.
